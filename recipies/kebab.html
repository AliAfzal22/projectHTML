How to Kebab?

Descriptions

Kebabs consist of cut up ground meat, sometimes with vegetables and various other accompaniments according to the specific recipe. Although kebabs are typically cooked on a skewer over a fire, some kebab dishes are oven-baked in a pan, or prepared as a stew such as tas kebab.

  1. Ingredients

    • 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
    • ½ teaspoon onion powder
    • ½ teaspoon dried Italian herb seasoning
    • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
    • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
    • 1 ¼ pounds ground lamb
